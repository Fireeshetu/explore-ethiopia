Lalibela

Lalibela, located in Ethiopia’s Amhara Region, is world-famous for its monolithic rock-hewn churches, many carved directly into the rock. It is a major pilgrimage site for Ethiopian Orthodox Christians.

Lalibela

Known as the “New Jerusalem,” Lalibela is home to eleven medieval monolithic churches, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Ethiopia’s greatest spiritual and historical treasures.
