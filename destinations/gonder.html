Fasil Ghebbi (Gondar)

Gondar is located about 725 km northwest of Addis Ababa. It's famous for Fasil Ghebbi, a royal fortress-city that was once Ethiopia’s capital under Emperor Fasilides.

Gondar

Gondar, often referred to as the "Camelot of Africa," is famous for its royal castles, ancient churches, and vibrant history as a former imperial capital.
