Sof Omar Cave

Sof Omar Cave is one of the longest cave systems in Africa, located in the East Bale Province of Oromia Region. It is famous for its stunning underground tunnels carved by the Web River.

Sof Omar Cave

The cave system is about 15.1 kilometres (9.4 mi) long and was discovered in 1897. It holds both geological and spiritual significance.
