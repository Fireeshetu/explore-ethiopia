Wenchi Crater Lake

Wenchi Crater Lake is a beautiful volcanic crater lake located about 126 km west of Addis Ababa. The lake lies within a caldera formed by an extinct volcano, with the highest surrounding peak reaching 3,450 meters (11,320 feet).
