General Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: October to March
  • Bring local currency (ETB)
  • Dress modestly in religious sites
  • Try local dishes like injera and doro wat
  • Respect cultural traditions and ask before taking photos

Tips for Lalibela

  • Visit on Tahesas 29 (January 7) to witness Ethiopian Christmas celebrations
  • Be prepared for hilly walks between churches
  • Selam Bus or domestic flights are recommended from Addis

Tips for Gondar

  • Visit during Timket (Epiphany) in January for grand processions
  • Sky Bus or Abay Bus can take you there from Addis
  • Book accommodation early during festival season

Tips for Aksum

  • St. Mary of Zion festival takes place in late November
  • Ethio Bus or flights are reliable options
  • Book in advance and pack for hot days and cool nights

Tips for Simien Mountains

  • Best time to hike is October–March
  • Wear layers and bring rain gear and snacks
  • Start from Gondar, then join a guided trek

Tips for Sof Omar Cave

  • Dry season (Oct–Mar) is best to avoid floods in the cave
  • Bring flashlights or headlamps and wear sturdy shoes
  • Use public buses to Bale then hire local guides

Tips for Wenchi Crater Lake

  • Ideal for day trips from Addis Ababa via Ambo
  • Wear hiking shoes and bring water/snacks
  • Local guides and horses are available for tours
